Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how ExteriorQuote ("the App"), developed by Joseph Joyner, handles information when you use the App.

Information We Collect

ExteriorQuote does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information with us or any third party. All data you enter — including company profile details, customer names, contact information, and quote details — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's local storage APIs.

Data Storage

All information is stored locally on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no cloud backend. Your data never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to share a quote PDF via text message or email using your device's native sharing features.

Sharing

When you send a quote PDF from the App, that action uses your device's built-in Mail or Messages app. The content of those messages is governed by Apple's privacy policy, not ours. We do not have access to any information you send.

Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporting services, or any third-party tracking tools. We do not track your usage in any way.

Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions, solely for the purposes described:

Photo Library — to allow you to add your company logo to quotes.
Camera — to allow you to photograph your company logo.
Contacts — optional, to auto-fill customer contact information.
None of the data accessed through these permissions is transmitted off your device or shared with us.

Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by business professionals and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
